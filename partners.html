How does ZASH work?

Depending on what type of store you have, Zash offers various Services. You can review the various Zash services in the User section .
Zash is integrated to the leading POS systems. If we haven't yet integrated Zash with Your POS system, let us know and we’ll take care of it. Alternatively Zash also offers a Zash B2B App, which is free to download on any Smartphone or Tablet.

Customers can locate merchants that accepts Zash through geolocation in the Zash App. Once you have a Zash business account set up, customers will be able to “Check In” to your store to make payments and much more!

Zash is fully secure and customers authorise the purchase each time in the Zash App. Your POS system will let you know once payment is authorised and the cashier can then close out the order. You will receive the funds next day, similar to a regular Card transaction process.

That’s it, once payment is completed you can hand over the final receipt to the Customer. You also have access to all the Client data such as Average check, demographics of Zash users at your store and so much more!
Security



The Zash transaction is done through a regular Banking Card Protocol. Its like paying with your card when you shop online. Payment is guaranteed by the bank and International card networks (such as Visa, MasterCard and Union Pay).

Zash payments are digitally encrypted. In addition Zash requires every payment to be authorized through a PIN or Fingerprint. The application can only be used after the UDID (Smartphone device) and MSISDN (SIM Card) has been verified that they are the same as when setting up the Zash App. This 4 step authentication model ensures that the transaction is more secure that regular card processing.

All data is fully protected. Information about the Payment is stored and processed only by the Bank (PCI DSS). If there is a refund, its handled exactly the same as in a Card Transaction.
